About Supreme Auto Spa

Hi, I’m Jakub Zielinski—I run Supreme Auto Spa. I’ve been valeting cars for years and just love getting them looking spotless. If it’s got four wheels and needs a freshen-up, I’m your guy.

I’m based in Knaphill and cover the surrounding areas. Whether it’s a quick outside wash or a full inside-and-out valet, I take my time and use decent products so your car leaves looking its best.

I’m also gearing up to add ceramic coating to the menu soon, so even more protection and gloss will be on offer.
